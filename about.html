ABOUT US

Edo Development Organization is an organizaytion that came to light in year 2016. the founders are group of likemiminded people who are today our Trustees.

Our Vision is to become the most prominent non gorvernmental body of influence in Edo state by the year 2028.

We exist to provide an effective platform for upwardly mobile Edo state stakeholdersto network and coordinate value creating efforts that impact Edo state and the lives of its indigenes.

In order to attain these, we have a well oriented and organized programme called "Impact your ward", which entails that;

  • Edo Development Organization (EDO) is a product of clear viision, genuine passion and irrevocable decisiion to contribute to the development of Edo State both at home and in Diasopora.
  • The development of Edo state is core to the mandate of EDO through holistic and mult-sectrorial approach.
  • Impact Your Ward programme is a buttom-up approach towards the development of of the people and the social welfare structures
  • Edo State iis a diverse and multi-cultural state and has high youth demography. Major occupation of residents are civil servants and farmers. The state economy has grown in recent hyears due to influx of investors and some infrastructal development however, a good percentage of iits population still live in poverty as well as the issues of youth development.
  • Also human trafficking and irregular migration is endemic in Edo state depleting the workforce population. This is also as a result of lack of basic social infrastructures and job opportunities especially in the rural areas.
  • To achieve a holistic rural socio-transformation programme, it is important to have structures in the LGAs and wards however EDO doesn't have structures in all the wards in terms of personnel
  • EDO intends to spread its tentacles accross the wards to effectively carry out a buttom-top approach through the IMPACT YOUR WARD.

Hence, our mission has kept us as outstanding the most effective platform that coordinates value creating effort that impact Edo state and the lives of her indigenes.
